Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Why Drag And Drop Feature? How It Will Help ?

We at Deep Thought, a Zero to One Innovation Company, support individual liberty. Here, the entire screen is thought of as the sky, where anything is possible. Every person's eyes and working style are unique, therefore we allow you the freedom to adjust your screen anyway you choose. We can't expect innovation if we restrict the space. The DT thon will alter how research and innovation are conducted, therefore participants' freedom to act anyway they see fit is crucial.

How Can Social Impact Of Selection Process Be Ensured?

We can create any process using the process builder tool DT thon. The selection process is a crucial component of the DT thon; if an applicant uses our platform, we ensure that transformation takes place whether they are chosen for a particular process or not. If he or she is not chosen during the selection process, we ensure through this platform that they receive the necessary guidance of course to make their path easy for the rest of their lives.

Why There Is No Deadline For Submission Of Processes?

In Deep Thought, we avoid using the word deadline and instead use milestones. Yet why? Why limit people's thinking by giving them a deadline? Why limit yourself to thinking inside the given time restrictions when you are making innovations? Participating in the DT thon process is not simple because each and every process is created using a lot of philosophy and thought. Therefore, we here grant the freedom to think independently to society's learners and thinkers.
